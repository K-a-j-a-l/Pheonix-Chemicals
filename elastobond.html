ElastoBond

ElastoBond is specially formulated acrylic polymer for modifying Cementitious compositions, Cement primers, Crack Filling, Waterproofing and Damp Proofing. Mortar prepared with ElastoBond is durable and flexible with elongation properties. It has excellent adhesion to various substrates like wood, concrete, brick and metal.

  • Bonding Agent, Cementitious Waterproofing.
  • Good workability at reduced water cement ratio.
  • Good bonding and adhesion to substrates.
  • High Tensile Strength.
  • Excellent Water impermeability.
  • For two component tile adhesives.

This product has good elongation properties with cement and cement mortar. It can be used for larger area like terrace waterproofing, basement waterproofing, podium slab waterproofing. It can also be used for sunken area, swimming pool area as waterproofing.
